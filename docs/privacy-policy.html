Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

GiveMeLeads ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address or Apple ID to create and manage your account.

Product Descriptions: You provide descriptions of your product or service to help us find relevant leads on Reddit.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used, scan frequency, and leads saved.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To search Reddit for relevant leads based on your product profile
  • To provide AI-powered lead scoring and reply suggestions
  • To improve the app experience and features
  • To communicate important updates about the service

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides enterprise-grade security including encryption at rest and in transit. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure you can only access your own data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Reddit Public API: We access publicly available Reddit content to find leads. We do not access private Reddit data.
  • Google Gemini AI: Lead analysis and reply generation (content sent is not stored by Google beyond processing)
  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication via Apple ID

5. Reddit Data

GiveMeLeads accesses publicly available Reddit posts and comments through Reddit's public JSON API. We do not:

  • Access your Reddit account
  • Post on your behalf
  • Collect private messages or non-public content

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings, which will permanently remove all your data including profiles, keywords, leads, and AI replies.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Export your lead data (available on paid plans)
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Children's Privacy

GiveMeLeads is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by email.
